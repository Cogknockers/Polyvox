# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is Polyvox a political party?
No. Polyvox is neutral infrastructure.

## Does Polyvox replace elections?
No. It prepares communities for better participation.

## Can officials participate?
Yes. Officials can claim profiles and respond publicly.

## Is Polyvox about write-in campaigns?
Write-ins are one possible outcome, not the foundation.

## Who owns Polyvox?
Polyvox is designed to be party-independent and auditable.
