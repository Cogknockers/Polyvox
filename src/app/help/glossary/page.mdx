# Glossary

## Reputation

A score that summarizes how the community has responded to your posts over time. It changes as votes and activity change.

## Trust

A human-readable tier based on reputation. It helps readers quickly understand the maturity of a profile without focusing on a single number.

## Vote weight

A display-only indicator that reflects voting balance. It does not change vote totals in v1.

## Downvote ratio

The share of a user's recent votes that are downvotes. This helps detect extreme voting patterns.
